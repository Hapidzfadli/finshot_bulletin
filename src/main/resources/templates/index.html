About This Board

Finshot Bulletin Board is a platform for community engagement and information sharing.

Our features include:

  • Create posts with a secure password
  • Browse and read community content
  • Edit or delete your own posts
  • Track post popularity through view counts

Join our growing community today!

How Finshot Bulletin Board Works
Browsing Posts

Visit the Posts page to see all available posts. Click on any post title to view its full content.

Creating a Post

To create a post:

  1. Click "Create Post" in the navigation menu
  2. Fill in your name, a title, and the content
  3. Create a password (you'll need this to edit or delete later)
  4. Click "Create Post" to publish
Editing or Deleting Posts

When viewing your post, you'll see Edit and Delete buttons. Clicking either will prompt you for the password you created when posting.

Post Guidelines
  • Titles: Maximum 50 Korean characters or 100 English characters
  • Author names: Maximum 10 characters
  • Be respectful and considerate of others in your posts
  • Inappropriate content may be removed
